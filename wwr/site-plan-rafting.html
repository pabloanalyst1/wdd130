Overview

Purpose

The website becomes a central hub for showcasing rafting services, facilitating easy booking and reservations, sharing informative content about safety measures and trip details, and encouraging customer interactions through reviews and feedback. This online platform not only enhances customer convenience but also strengthens the business's visibility, credibility, and competitiveness in the adventure tourism market.

Audience

Target Audience: We cater to adventure enthusiasts aged 18-45, with middle to upper-middle income, seeking thrilling outdoor experiences such as water rafting and other outdoor activities. Our audience is tech-savvy and likely to access our website primarily via mobile devices for quick booking and information retrieval. What do we want? - We seek exciting and adventurous experiences in natural settings, such as water rafting trips, to challenge ourselves and enjoy memorable outdoor activities. What do we expect to find or what questions will be answered by accessing the website? - We expect to find detailed information about our rafting trips, including trip durations, safety measures, equipment provided, pricing, and booking options. We also want to see photos and testimonials from previous participants to gauge the experience. How will our audience usually access our site (desktop, laptop, mobile device)? - Our audience is likely to access our site via mobile devices such as smartphones and tablets for convenience and quick access while on the go or during leisure time. Therefore, our website needs to be mobile-friendly and easy to navigate on smaller screens.
